SaaS Product

Stackr

Conversion-focused waitlist landing page with Mailchimp and launch-ready polish.

The Challenge

What the business needed.

The product was in private beta. They needed a marketing page that looked launch-ready — not like a Notion doc with a logo on top.

Every visitor was a potential waitlist signup. Friction and vagueness were the enemy.

The Approach

How it was solved.

Conversion-focused landing page: product screenshots, feature section, FAQ, and a waitlist form wired to Mailchimp.

OG tags and social sharing were set so founders could drop the link on Twitter without it looking unfinished.

A minimal CMS setup lets the team update the launch-date banner without calling a developer every time the timeline shifts.
