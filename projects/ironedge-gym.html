Fitness and Gym

IronEdge Gym

Multi-location gym site with class schedules, memberships, and trainer profiles.

The Challenge

What the business needed.

The gym opened a second location. The website still only showed the first. No class schedule, no membership info — people had to DM for basics.

A gym site should feel energetic without looking chaotic. They needed both locations, schedules, and pricing — cleanly.

The Approach

How it was solved.

Multi-location WordPress build. Dynamic class schedule via styled Google Calendar embed, membership pricing page, and trainer profiles that feel human.

Visual energy came from photography and motion — not from clutter. Video background was performance-optimized so it does not punish mobile visitors.

Both locations share a clear structure so locals can find hours, classes, and how to join without guessing.
