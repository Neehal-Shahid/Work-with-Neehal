Dental Clinic

ClearSmile Dental

A calm clinic redesign with booking, service FAQs, and a 98 performance score.

The Challenge

What the business needed.

The existing website had not been updated in six years. It was slow, not mobile-friendly, and had no online booking — patients were calling and hanging up.

In healthcare, trust is the product. The old site did not feel like a place you would trust with your teeth.

The Approach

How it was solved.

Full redesign with a calm palette and clear service structure. Booking integration, FAQ-backed service pages, and a before/after gallery that feels clinical — not salesy.

Contact forms were structured with care around sensitive information. The priority was clarity and professionalism over flashy animation.

Performance was treated as a trust signal. A clinic site that loads slowly feels careless. We pushed for a 98 Lighthouse score and kept it.
